MONTHLY PUZZLE PROMPT

“A Narrow Escape”

For this prompt, design a puzzle that involved some sort of escape or other connectivity mechanic. This could be a puzzle genre that involves drawing a path to an exit point such as Icebarn, or a shading genre on an unusually shaped grid. Or, adapt a Sudoku variant to meet the theme. If you decide to make a puzzle designed around connectivity restrictions, there is no requirement for this to be obvious from the start, provided it emerges as a key step along the solve path.
